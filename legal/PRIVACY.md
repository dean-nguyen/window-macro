## 1. What we collect

**Locally on your device (not sent to us):**
- Your macros and template images, stored under
  `%APPDATA%/WindowMacroBotData/`.
- A signed license cache (`license.json`) so the app works briefly offline.

**Sent to our license provider ({{LICENSE_PROVIDER}}) during activation and
periodic re-validation of a paid license:**
- Your **license key**.
- A **hardware ID** — an anonymised, one-way fingerprint of your machine used to
  lock the license to your device (it does not identify you personally).
- Your **IP address** and basic request metadata, as part of normal network
  communication and abuse prevention.

**Collected by your payment provider ({{PAYMENT_PROVIDER}}) at purchase:**
- Order and contact details (e.g. email) and payment information. This is handled
  by that provider under their own privacy policy; we receive only what is needed
  to fulfil and support your order.

## 2. Why we collect it

- To activate, validate, and protect paid licenses.
- To prevent fraud, key sharing, and abuse.
- To provide support and deliver updates.

The free tier makes **no** licensing network calls.

## 3. Legal basis (where applicable)

Performance of our contract with you (delivering the licensed software) and our
legitimate interest in preventing fraud and protecting our product.

## 4. Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with the service providers
above ({{LICENSE_PROVIDER}}, {{PAYMENT_PROVIDER}}) as needed to run the product,
and where required by law.

## 5. Retention

We keep license and order records for as long as needed to provide the service,
support you, and meet legal/accounting obligations, then delete or anonymise them.
